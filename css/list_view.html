Iztaccihuatl Hazards Details Page

Volcano Description

This volcano is located on the border between the State of Mexico and Puebla. The elevation is 5,320 meters tall and last erupted in the year 1868. The first person to ever ascend this mountain was James de Salis. It is the eighth highest mountain peek of North America.

Key Details

  • Elevation = 5,320 meters
  • last erupted = 1868
  • Location = border of State of Mexico and Puebla
  • Third highest in Mexico
